Warehouse Delivery Driver
Non-Exempt/Full-Time
Branch Manager
Position Summary:
Working at the direction of the Branch Manager, the warehouse delivery driver is responsible for
providing support to the warehousing operations with specific responsibilities of transporting
orders over designated routes; ensuring safe operation of vehicles; and loading and unloading
orders.
Subordinate Summary:
This position has no formal supervisory responsibilities.
Essential Job Results and Responsibilities:
Core Duties and Responsibilities
1. Assists in receiving stock and nonstock items for the purpose of ensuring specifications,
quantity and quality of orders are correct.
2. Cleans warehouse for the purpose of maintaining a safe and sanitary work area.
3. Delivers a variety of materials primarily to customers for the purpose of distributing
inventory from internal or external sources over scheduled and/or unscheduled routes to
assigned locations and/or individuals.
4. Drives vehicles (e.g. truck, van, forklift, etc.) for the purpose of transporting orders and
materials to designated sites.
5. Loads and unloads delivery trucks for the purpose of receiving stock and/or filling orders
for transport.
6. Maintains delivery vehicles (e.g. checking fluid levels, fueling, cleaning, etc.) for the
purpose of ensuring safe operation of vehicle.
7. Maintains records (e.g. delivery log, etc.) for the purpose of documenting activities and
providing reliable resource information.
8. Participates in physical inventories for the purpose of verifying stock and identifying
losses.
9. Performs all functions within the scope of the warehouse for the purpose of providing
warehousing operations.
10. Prepares orders by pulling from stock for the purpose of meeting delivery requirements.
