Customer Service Results and Responsibilities
1. Assist sales efforts on an as needed basis including but not limited to assisting in-store
customers, filling customer orders, delivery to customers, and answering telephones.
2. Answers incoming telephone calls and emails and greets customers who walk in the store.
Handles multiple customers and customer requests simultaneously while maintaining a
friendly and professional manner.
3. Follows up promptly on all customer requests for information, quotes, alternative parts and
delivery dates. Proactively communicates with customers about any expected delays or
issues with their order.
4. Develops good working relationships and rapport with customers. Greets regular
customers by name.
5. Maintains the highest standards in cleanliness and appearance of the showroom through
stocking shelves, housekeeping, creating displays, etc.
General Results Responsibilities
1. Maintains knowledge of new and existing products and inventory by participating in training,
studying Johnstone and industry catalogs and web sites, and seeking knowledge from
experienced staff members and factory representatives.
2. Is organized and orderly, and capable of maintaining appropriate records.
3. Completes all transactions and necessary paperwork accurately and in a timely manner.
4. May represent Johnstone Supply at trade associations, events and local chapters.
Johnstone Supply believes that each employee's contribution should not be limited by the assigned
responsibilities. Therefore, it is expected that each employee will offer his/her services wherever
and whenever necessary to ensure the success of our endeavors.
Qualifications:
To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential job
function satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skills,
and/or abilities required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with
disabilities to perform the essential functions.
Education and Experience:
⚫ Required: High School Diploma or G.E.D and minimum of two years related experience
and/or training in material handling of durable goods including hazardous materials; OR
equivalent combination of education and experience.
⚫ Preferred: The above plus 2 years experience with a wholesale HVAC/R or similar
industry.
Job-Specific Knowledge, Skills & Abilities
Ability to provide consistent, professional customer service.
⚫ Problem-solving skills: listening to, interpreting, and meeting the needs of others;
diagnosing and rectifying problems that will often require attention to detail.
• Ability to maintain a positive, courteous, and friendly demeanor.
